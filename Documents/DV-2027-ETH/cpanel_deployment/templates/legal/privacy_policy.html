1. Information We Collect

DV Portal Ethiopia collects information necessary to assist Ethiopian citizens with their Diversity Visa applications. The information we collect is similar to what is required by the US Department of State's official DV program.

1.1 Personal Information

  • Full name (first, middle, last)
  • Date and place of birth
  • Gender and marital status
  • Contact information (email, phone number, address)
  • Educational background and work history
  • Passport and identification information
  • Family information (spouse and children details)

1.2 Documents and Photos

  • Passport-style photographs meeting US DV requirements
  • Identity documents and certificates
  • Educational certificates and work experience documentation
  • Payment receipts and proof of payment

1.3 Technical Information

  • IP address and browser information
  • Device information and operating system
  • Login times and session data
  • Application usage and navigation patterns

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for the purposes of:

  • Processing your Diversity Visa application assistance request
  • Validating documents and photos against US requirements
  • Communicating with you about your application status
  • Providing customer support and technical assistance
  • Improving our services and user experience
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

3.1 US Government Submission

With your explicit consent, we submit your completed application to the US Department of State's official Diversity Visa entry system. This is the primary purpose of our service.

3.2 Service Providers

We may share information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our platform, such as:

  • Cloud hosting services (for secure data storage)
  • AI validation services (for photo and document checking)
  • Payment processing services (for fee collection)
  • Communication services (for Telegram notifications)

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, regulation, or court order, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Encrypted database storage
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and user authentication
  • Secure backup and disaster recovery procedures

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for the duration necessary to provide our services and as required by law:

  • Application Data: Retained for 7 years after application submission
  • Payment Records: Retained for 7 years for accounting purposes
  • Communication Records: Retained for 3 years
  • Technical Logs: Retained for 1 year

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Request transfer of your information
  • Objection: Object to processing of your information

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience, analyze usage patterns, and maintain security. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

8. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with third-party services:

  • Google Gemini AI: For application and photo validation
  • Telegram API: For notifications and communication
  • Payment Processors: For secure payment processing

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside Ethiopia, including the United States, for the purpose of DV application submission. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you are under 18, please do not use our services or provide any information to us.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes via email and through our platform. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@polocash.com
  • Telegram: @dv2027apply
  • Address: DV Portal Ethiopia, Addis Ababa, Ethiopia
Important Disclaimer

DV Portal Ethiopia is an independent service provider and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the US Department of State or the US Government. We provide assistance services for Ethiopian citizens applying to the official US Diversity Visa Program.
