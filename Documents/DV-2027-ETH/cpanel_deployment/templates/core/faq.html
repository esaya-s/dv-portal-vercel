DV Portal Ethiopia FAQ

Find answers to common questions about our DV application assistance service.

DV Portal Ethiopia is a professional service that assists Ethiopian citizens with their US Diversity Visa (Green Card Lottery) applications. We help ensure your application meets all requirements and is submitted correctly.

No, we are an independent service provider and are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the US Department of State or the US Government. We provide assistance services for Ethiopian citizens applying to the official US Diversity Visa Program.

You can contact our support team via:
  • Email: support@dvportal-ethiopia.com
  • Telegram: @anvilutech
  • Phone: +251-911-XXXXXX
  • Contact form on our website

Once payment is verified, we typically process applications within 2-3 business days. The entire DV registration period is usually from October to November each year.

You will receive a confirmation notification via:
  • Telegram message (if you've set up Telegram notifications)
  • Email confirmation
  • Status update in your online dashboard
Each application receives a unique confirmation number in the format DV2027-XXXXXXXXXXX.

You can edit your application only if it's in "Draft" or "Payment Pending" status. Once your application has been reviewed and submitted to the US DV system, no further edits are possible.

The 800 ETB fee covers our professional services including application review, AI-powered validation, photo verification, application submission, and ongoing support. This is NOT a payment to the US government.

We currently accept payments only through Commercial Bank of Ethiopia (CBE). After making your payment, you'll need to upload proof of payment (receipt or screenshot) with your application.

Refunds are provided only in the following circumstances:
  • Technical failure on our part preventing application submission
  • Duplicate payment errors
  • Service cancellation by us before processing begins
No refunds will be provided for change of mind, incorrect information provided by you, or non-selection in the DV lottery.

Ethiopian citizens and those born in Ethiopia are eligible. You must have a high school education or equivalent, OR two years of work experience in a job requiring training. You must also meet other US immigration requirements.

You must have either:
  • A high school education or equivalent (12 years of formal education), OR
  • Two years of work experience within the past five years in an occupation requiring at least two years of training or experience

Yes, you must include your spouse and all unmarried children under 21 years of age in your application, whether they are living with you or not, and whether they will immigrate with you or not. Failure to list all eligible family members can result in disqualification.

Photos must have a white background, show your full face looking directly at camera, be recent (within 6 months), and meet specific size and quality requirements. Our AI system will validate your photo before submission.

To set up Telegram notifications:
  1. Install Telegram app on your phone
  2. Search for our bot: @DVPortalEthiopiaBot
  3. Start a chat and click "Start"
  4. Use the /verify command with your phone number to link your account

We accept the following file formats:
  • Photos: JPEG (.jpg) format only
  • Documents: PDF, JPG, or PNG
  • Payment proof: JPG, PNG, or PDF
Maximum file size is 5MB per file.
