About DV Portal Ethiopia

Our Mission

Helping Ethiopian citizens achieve their American dream through professional DV application assistance.

DV Portal Ethiopia was established to provide a reliable, secure, and accessible platform for Ethiopian citizens to apply for the US Diversity Visa Program. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to helping you navigate the application process with ease and confidence.

We understand the challenges that Ethiopian applicants face when applying for the DV program, including internet connectivity issues, photo requirements, and form complexities. Our service addresses these challenges by offering a comprehensive solution that combines technology with personalized support.

Important Disclaimer

DV Portal Ethiopia is an independent service provider and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the US Department of State or the US Government. We provide assistance services for Ethiopian citizens applying to the official US Diversity Visa Program.

Our service fee (800 ETB) covers application assistance, AI validation, and submission support. This is separate from any fees that may be required by the US government if you are selected in the DV lottery.

While we strive to provide the best assistance possible, we cannot guarantee selection in the DV lottery or visa approval, as these decisions are made solely by the US Department of State.
