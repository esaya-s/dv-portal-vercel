Education or Work Experience

You must meet ONE of the following requirements:

Education Requirement

You must have completed a 12-year course of elementary and secondary education in Ethiopia or another country.

Work Experience Requirement

You must have two years of work experience within the past five years in an occupation that requires at least two years of training or experience to perform.

The U.S. Department of Labor's O*Net Online database is used to determine qualifying work experience. The occupation must be classified in Job Zone 4 or 5, and have a Specific Vocational Preparation (SVP) rating of 7.0 or higher.

Family Eligibility

If you are selected, your immediate family members may also receive visas to accompany you to the United States:

  • Spouse: Your legally married husband or wife
  • Children: Unmarried children under 21 years of age

Important: You must list ALL eligible family members on your application, even if they do not plan to immigrate with you. Failure to do so may result in disqualification.

Additional Requirements

If selected, you must also meet these additional requirements:

Valid Passport

You must have a valid, unexpired passport at the time of your visa interview.

Medical Examination

You must undergo a medical examination by an authorized physician.

No Criminal Record

You must not have been convicted of certain crimes or violations.

Financial Support

You must demonstrate that you will not become a public charge in the US.
