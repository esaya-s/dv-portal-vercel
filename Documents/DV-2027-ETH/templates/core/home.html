About the Diversity Visa Program

The Diversity Visa (DV) Program is a United States government program that provides up to 55,000 immigrant visas annually to people from countries with low rates of immigration to the United States.

Global Opportunity

Open to citizens of countries with low immigration rates to the United States

Education Requirements

High school education or equivalent work experience required

Family Inclusion

Include spouse and unmarried children under 21 in your application

Quick Facts

  • Program Year: DV-2027
  • Total Visas: Up to 55,000
  • Registration Period: Oct-Nov 2025
  • Selection Method: Random Computer Drawing
  • Application Fee: 800 ETB
